# Project: Huzur

## Vision
Huzur is a premium spiritual and mindfulness application designed to provide peace and tranquility to its users. The goal is to build a faultless, high-quality app that "wows" users with its design and eventually generates sustainable revenue through premium features and integrations.

## Core Value
Providing a premium, reliable, and aesthetically delightful user experience for spiritual well-being.

## Target Audience
Users seeking mindfulness, prayer guidance, and spiritual peace who appreciate high-quality, premium design.

## Strategy: Design & Monetization
- **Visual Excellence**: Implement a "WOW" factor design using modern typography, smooth gradients, and micro-animations.
- **Reliability**: Ensure the app is bug-free and stable across all platforms (Web & Android).
- **Monetization**: Integrate monetization pathways (AdMob, RevenueCat) that do not compromise the premium feel.

## Requirements

### Validated (Existing)
- ✓ React/Vite/Capacitor basic setup.
- ✓ Firebase integration (Auth, Firestore, Messaging).
- ✓ Core features implemented: Quran, Prayer Times, Dua, Hatim.
- ✓ Multi-language support (TR, EN, AR).
- ✓ AdMob & RevenueCat (purchases) plugins integrated.

### Active (Hypotheses)
- [ ] Premium Design Overhaul: Refactor core UI to a "Premium/Wow" aesthetic.
- [ ] Reliability Pass: Solve lingering Gradle/Playwright issues and stabilize the codebase.
- [ ] UX Optimization: Smoother transitions, better navigation, and micro-interactions.
- [ ] Monetization Strategy Implementation: Optimize ad placement and subscription conversion screens (Paywalls).

### Out of Scope
- Building a social network (focus is on individual well-being).
- Developing custom server-side infrastructure (using Firebase for everything).
